Helpdesk

Please use this portal to log an incident for any issues you face or observe. Examples are System not working, Internet / Wi-Fi issues, UPS, Electrical / Plumbing, RO Water, Intercom, AC, Transport, Attendance, Cafeteria, etc. The process is simple.

1) Sign in using Digital ID Credentials (same as LMS/ERP).

2) If you forgot your password, use this link - https://passwordreset.microsoftonline.com/

3) Steps for Incident Logging:

  1. Click "Sign In"
  2. Sign in using your Digital ID credentials
  3. Select the appropriate Help Topic
  4. Fill in all the mandatory fields
  5. Click "Submit"

4) To check the status/updates to your tickets, click on "Veiw Tickets" on the menu bar.
